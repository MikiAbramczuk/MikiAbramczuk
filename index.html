who am i?

Hi, my name is Miki Abramczuk.
I’m a 20-year-old living in Warsaw, Poland.

For as long as I can remember, I have always tried to stand out from the crowd through my clothing or behavior.
I have always paid attention to the beauty of the surrounding nature and appreciated simplicity and minimalism.
When designing websites, I try to get into the skin of a potential client and understand their needs. My sites combine good design and what is probably most important for business owners - good conversions.
In my free time I run, cook, learn languages and play the piano.
I love meeting new inspiring people. (ideally when working with them)

why webflow?

Webflow simply destroys the competition

Webflow destroys the competition
Why?
Because it writes the code for you allowing  to create incredible websites without ANY limitations.
It gives you virtually all the possibilities of coding without having to learn it for years. On top of that, it guarantees uninterruptible servers, an easy-to-use CMS and an editing mode, so the site owner can modify the site without having to constantly contact its developer.
An amazing community to help solve any problem is just the icing on the cake.

my workflow

I try to keep my workflow as uncluttered as possible.

Reliability of cooperation, great communication and transparency of the contract are very important to me.
I know very well that designing and creating websites can be something very exotic for clients, so I guarantee understanding and patience.The outline of my usual work process is as follows:

P1: Discovery Call & Project Brief (Establishing goals, understanding the target audience).
P2: Design Part in Figma (Moodboard and Wireframe).
P3: Development Part in Webflow (Responsiveness, Transparent Code, CMS)

There are three free revisions for each step, only after one step is approved I start working on the next one.
